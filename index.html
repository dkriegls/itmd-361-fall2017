ITMD 361: Fall 2017 Fundamentals of Web Development

This class covers the foundational principles of web development. How to research client-server architecture according to current open standards. Taught by Professor Daniel Krieglstein. Course developed by IIT Professors Brian Bailey, Dr. Karl Stolley, & Daniel Krieglstein.
